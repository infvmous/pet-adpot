Why we recommend adopting

There are numerous advantages to adopting a pet, such as adding a special member to the family, reduced costs & helping out an animal in need. However, here are the facts - millions of pets enter shelters each year.
And hundreds of thousands are euthanized each year. We're not trying to guilt you or put you down, but adoption is something that is important to us. We understand that there are a lot of things to consider, however we would really appreciate it if you decide to adopt.

We understand that everyone's pet parenting journey is different and we want to make adoption even easier for you. So if you're here, it's great! Don't worry though, we won't judge if you find your pet another way. We just want to be here for support however we can.

How to find the right pet

Shelters are filled with pets that need your love! Adopting comes with so many benefits - it's a heartwarming experience, it will save you money and most of all, you'll be doing something incredibly selfless. Despite this, the reality is that millions of pets find themselves in shelters each year.

And hundreds of thousands are euthanized each year. We're not trying to put any pressure on you, but we want you to understand how important adoption is to us. So we would love it if you considered adopting. And, since you're here, we're guessing you are. Seriously, no judgment if you find a pet another way (every pet parent journey is different!). br But we're here to help make adoption easier, however we can.

Get to know us

We can certainly relate to the pet adoption process as most of us here have gone through it ourselves. It's definitely a rewarding experience but we understand that it's quite a lengthy and paperwork-filled process.

We want to make pet parenting as easy as possible, so we provide the tools and advice you need, and make everything transparent for you. This helps throughout the entire process of finding the right pet to raising it happily every day.

We're devoted to helping millions of people and pets unite with each other, no matter what it takes.
